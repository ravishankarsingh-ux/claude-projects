The manifesto

Stop searching.
Start knowing.

Every organization is sitting on an answer to almost every question it will ever ask itself — buried across Slack threads, forgotten docs, and the heads of people who've moved on. Devadigm doesn't index your workplace. It understands it.

We built a system that reasons across your entire knowledge graph the way a trusted colleague would — with context, memory, and judgment.

How it works

One question.
Every source.

Ask in plain language. Devadigm reasons across your docs, tickets, and conversations in real time, and hands back a single, cited answer — not a list of links.

Always in sync

It learns as your
company moves.

No stale wikis. No re-indexing. Devadigm watches your workplace change and keeps its understanding current, so the answer you get today reflects the decision made an hour ago.
